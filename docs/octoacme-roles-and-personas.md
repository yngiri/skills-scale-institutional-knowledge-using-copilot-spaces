# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## Product Owner

### Role Summary
Product Owners define and prioritize product requirements, represent end-users, and collaborate with stakeholders and delivery teams to ensure the right features are built.

### Responsibilities
- Define and prioritize product requirements and user stories
- Represent end-user needs and perspectives
- Collaborate with stakeholders to gather requirements
- Work closely with delivery teams to clarify requirements
- Accept or reject completed work based on acceptance criteria
- Maintain and groom the product backlog

### Goals
- Deliver maximum value to end-users
- Ensure product features align with user needs
- Maintain a well-prioritized and clear backlog

### Typical Communication
- Daily availability for team questions
- Backlog refinement sessions
- Sprint reviews and demos
- Stakeholder updates on product progress

### Interactions with Other Roles
Works closely with Product Managers on vision and strategy, collaborates with Developers on implementation details, coordinates with Project Managers on delivery timelines.

---

## Agile Coach

### Role Summary
Agile Coaches facilitate agile best practices, support continuous improvement, and mentor teams and project managers on agile methodologies and processes.

### Responsibilities
- Facilitate agile ceremonies (standups, retrospectives, planning)
- Coach teams on agile principles and practices
- Identify and remove organizational impediments
- Mentor Project Managers and team leads
- Drive continuous improvement initiatives
- Support team dynamics and collaboration

### Goals
- Improve team velocity and efficiency
- Foster a culture of continuous improvement
- Increase team autonomy and self-organization

### Typical Communication
- Facilitation of team ceremonies
- One-on-one coaching sessions
- Team retrospectives and improvement workshops
- Organizational agile transformation updates

### Interactions with Other Roles
Coaches Project Managers and teams, works with all roles to improve processes, collaborates with leadership on organizational improvements.

---

## Business Analyst

### Role Summary
Business Analysts elicit requirements, document user stories, and bridge between technical teams and business stakeholders to ensure clear understanding of needs.

### Responsibilities
- Elicit and analyze business requirements
- Document user stories and acceptance criteria
- Create process flows and diagrams
- Bridge communication between business and technical teams
- Validate that solutions meet business needs
- Support impact analysis for changes

### Goals
- Ensure clear, complete requirements
- Minimize rework due to unclear specifications
- Maximize alignment between business needs and technical solutions

### Typical Communication
- Requirements workshops with stakeholders
- User story refinement with teams
- Documentation and specification updates
- Clarification sessions during development

### Interactions with Other Roles
Works with Product Managers and Product Owners on requirements, collaborates with Developers to clarify technical details, supports Project Managers with scope definition.

---

## QA Lead

### Role Summary
QA Leads ensure quality standards, plan and coordinate testing activities, and work with developers and product owners to deliver high-quality outputs.

### Responsibilities
- Define and maintain quality standards
- Plan and coordinate testing strategies and activities
- Lead test automation initiatives
- Review test coverage and quality metrics
- Coordinate with developers on defect resolution
- Validate acceptance criteria are testable

### Goals
- Maintain high product quality and reliability
- Reduce production defects
- Improve test coverage and automation

### Typical Communication
- Daily coordination with development teams
- Test planning and strategy sessions
- Quality metrics reporting
- Defect triage meetings

### Interactions with Other Roles
Collaborates closely with Developers on testing strategies, works with Product Owners to validate acceptance criteria, coordinates with Project Managers on quality gates and release readiness.

---

## Stakeholder Representatives

### Role Summary
Stakeholder Representatives represent specific business units or customer perspectives, communicate priorities, and ensure alignment between project objectives and stakeholder expectations.

### Responsibilities
- Represent business unit or customer interests
- Communicate priorities and constraints
- Provide feedback on proposed solutions
- Participate in key decision points
- Validate that deliverables meet stakeholder needs
- Facilitate stakeholder communication and alignment

### Goals
- Ensure project outcomes meet stakeholder needs
- Maintain stakeholder engagement and satisfaction
- Provide timely feedback and decisions

### Typical Communication
- Regular stakeholder updates and reviews
- Feedback sessions on demos and prototypes
- Priority setting and trade-off discussions
- Executive briefings and status reports

### Interactions with Other Roles
Provides input to Product Managers and Product Owners on priorities, gives feedback to Project Managers on progress, validates outcomes with all delivery roles.
